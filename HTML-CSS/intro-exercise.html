Peanut Butter 'n' Jelly

Ingredients

  • Peanut Butter
  • Jelly
  • 2 slices of Bread

Description

A basic PB&J sandwitch.

Instructions

  1. Use a butter knife to spread peanut butter evenly on one bread slice
  2. Use a butter knife to spread jelly on the other bread slice
  3. Place the two bread slices together to create your masterpiece
